LEXGUARD CATEGORY COVERAGE AGREEMENT
Agreement Date: January 1, 2026. This Category Coverage Agreement is entered into between Alpha Client Inc. and Beta Vendor LLC as the parties. The agreement becomes effective on January 1, 2026, commences immediately, and the initial term expires on December 31, 2026 unless extended.

RENEWAL AND REFUNDS
The subscription renews automatically for successive one-year periods unless cancelled at least sixty days before the renewal date. Fees are non-refundable, no refunds will be issued, and the customer pays even where the customer does not use the service.

GOVERNING LAW AND FORUM
This agreement is governed by the laws of the State of New York, and any jurisdiction or venue dispute may be brought in the provider's home county or in a court of its choosing selected by the company.

MOST FAVORED PRICING
Vendor shall provide Customer with most favored customer status, better terms, and no less favorable pricing than Vendor provides to any similarly situated customer.

NON COMPETE AND OUTSIDE WORK
For twenty-four months after termination, Contractor shall not compete with Customer, shall not work for, advise, consult with, invest in, or assist any competitive business or anticipated product line anywhere in the world. Contractor shall devote services exclusively to the company and shall not engage in any outside business, outside work, teaching, consulting, open-source, advisory, or creative activity without prior written approval.

EXCLUSIVITY AND CUSTOMER NON SOLICIT
Vendor grants Customer exclusive rights and will work exclusively with Customer for this market. Vendor shall not license, sell, collaborate, or work with any third party, and shall not solicit any customer, client, partner, or customer of the counterparty.

COMPETITIVE RESTRICTION EXCEPTION
The restrictions above shall not apply to passive investment of less than two percent in a public company, and this carve-out is a permitted exception to the non-compete and exclusivity restrictions.

EMPLOYEE NON SOLICIT AND NON DISPARAGEMENT
During the term and for two years after termination, each party shall not solicit any employee or contractor of the other party, hire any employee or contractor, or make any disparaging, non-disparagement, or negative statement about the other party.

TERMINATION RIGHTS
Customer may terminate for convenience immediately and may terminate this agreement at any time without cause. Vendor may terminate only after providing ninety days notice and a thirty day cure period, and Customer may terminate the project at any time without paying for partially completed work.

FIRST REFUSAL AND CHANGE OF CONTROL
Customer has a right of first refusal, right of first offer, and right of first negotiation for any sale of the product line. A change of control, merger, stock sale, transfer of substantially all assets, or assignment by operation of law triggers Customer approval rights.

ANTI ASSIGNMENT
Vendor may not assign this agreement, shall not assign any rights, and may not transfer this agreement without Customer's prior written consent to assignment.

REVENUE AND PRICING
Vendor will pay a royalty and revenue share equal to ten percent of gross receipts and will share profit from the covered product. Vendor accepts a price restriction, may not raise price or reduce price without approval, and any price increase requires Customer consent.

MINIMUM COMMITMENT AND VOLUME
Customer has a minimum commitment, minimum purchase, minimum spend, and minimum units order every quarter. If usage exceeds the threshold, the volume restriction applies and an overage fee increase will be charged.

POST TERMINATION SERVICES

MUTUAL SERVICES AGREEMENT
This Mutual Services Agreement is between Harbor Foods LLC and Slate Systems Pvt. Ltd. Services begin on June 1, 2026.

SERVICES
Slate Systems will provide support services described in each signed statement of work. New tasks, deadlines, or security requirements require written agreement by both parties.

PAYMENT
Harbor Foods will pay undisputed invoices within thirty days. If Harbor Foods ends a statement of work early, it will pay for completed services and approved expenses through the end date.

SECURITY REVIEW
Harbor Foods may review relevant service records once per year with ten business days written notice. Reviews occur during business hours and must protect Slate Systems confidential information.

LIABILITY
Each party is responsible for direct damages caused by its own breach. Neither party is responsible for speculative, remote, or punitive damages.

INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
Harbor Foods owns final deliverables created only for Harbor Foods after payment. Slate Systems keeps background tools, reusable methods, general know-how, and pre-existing materials.

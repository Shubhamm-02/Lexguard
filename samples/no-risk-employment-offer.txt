BALANCED EMPLOYMENT OFFER
This offer letter is between Clearpath Analytics and Dev Mehta. Employment starts on May 1, 2026.

ROLE AND WORK
Dev will serve as a data analyst. The company expects good-faith performance during working hours and will discuss workload changes in writing.

SIDE PROJECTS
Dev may continue personal projects, teaching, open-source contributions, passive investments, and creative work that do not use company confidential information or company equipment.

INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
The company owns work created specifically for assigned company projects during employment. Dev keeps prior inventions, general skills, personal templates, public portfolio material, and work created outside assigned duties.

CONFIDENTIALITY
Dev will protect marked confidential business information for three years after disclosure. Public information, prior knowledge, and independently developed information are excluded.

ENDING EMPLOYMENT
Either side may end employment with two weeks written notice where practical. The company will pay all earned salary and approved expenses through the final workday.

BALANCED CONSULTING AGREEMENT
This Balanced Consulting Agreement is between GreenDesk Labs and Nisha Patel. The agreement starts on April 1, 2026 and ends on June 30, 2026 unless both parties sign an extension.

SERVICES
Consultant will provide product research and weekly recommendations. The parties will confirm scope changes in writing before extra work begins.

PAYMENT
Client will pay fixed milestone fees within fifteen days after receiving an invoice for completed work. If Client ends the project early, Client will pay for work completed through the end date.

INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
Client owns the final deliverables after payment. Consultant keeps pre-existing tools, templates, skills, general know-how, and portfolio rights for non-confidential work samples.

CONFIDENTIALITY
Each party will protect marked confidential information for three years. Confidentiality does not include public information, independently developed information, or information already known before disclosure.

DISPUTES
The parties will first meet remotely to resolve any dispute. If unresolved, either party may bring a claim in a mutually convenient court.

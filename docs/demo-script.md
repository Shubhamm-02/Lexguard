# LEXGUARD Demo Script

## 90-Second Pitch

LEXGUARD is not a contract summarizer. It is a contract risk cockpit. It extracts clauses, identifies harmful language, reasons across clauses, explains the real-world consequence for the affected party, and turns the result into negotiation-ready asks.

## Demo Path

1. Open the Employment Agreement sample.
2. Click Analyze.
3. Show the overall score, risk radar, and top finding.
4. Select "Restrictive Non-Compete" and point to the evidence snippet.
5. Show the real-world implication and negotiation ask.
6. Scroll to Adversarial Review Board to show multiple reasoning perspectives.
7. Show Scenario Impact to explain why this matters after termination or dispute.
8. Switch to Subscription Terms to demonstrate privacy and renewal risks.
9. Explain that Vertex AI Gemini can be enabled as a second reviewer in the same GCP project.

## Judge Line

LEXGUARD stands out because it converts contract text into practical risk decisions: evidence, impact, negotiation, scenario, and explainability in one workflow.
